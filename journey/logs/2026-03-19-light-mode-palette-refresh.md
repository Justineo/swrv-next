# Light mode palette refresh

- Replaced the previous light-mode palette with a fresh warm-paper palette.
- Kept the change scoped to the light-mode `--theme-*` tokens in the VitePress theme stylesheet.
- New direction:
  - paper background instead of stark white
  - darker pine ink for headings
  - muted stone body text and borders
  - verdigris primary accent for hero, buttons, and links
- Validated the result against the built site in-browser before committing.
